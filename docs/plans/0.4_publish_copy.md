## Itch.io Page Copy

### Short Description

A single-player casino roguelike about debt, heat, crooked tables, and one last
climb to the Grand Casino.

### Long Description

Beat the House is a run-based casino roguelike built in Godot. Start from a
rough home base, pack a small loadout, travel through a seeded world map, buy
strange items, take services or loans when the spiral gets tight, and play full
simulations of Pull Tabs, Slots, Bar Dice, Blackjack, Baccarat, Roulette, and
Video Poker.

Every hand, spin, drink, cheat, lender, travel choice, and bad exit changes the
run state. Win clean by reaching the Grand Casino and cashing out under the
heat line, or survive the back-room showdown when the house decides you have
become a problem.

Between runs, open collection bags, manage storage, upgrade from the alley to
better housing, and sell spare finds at the pawn counter. This is Act 1's
completion release, with the future boss fight/final scene deliberately saved
for later.

This is simulated gambling only. There is no real-money wagering, no cash
prize, and no gambling monetization.
