## Changelog (itch devlog body)

Beat the House v0.4 — Home, Loadout, Time, New Content

- Get a home of your own and upgrade it
- Bring your favorite items into each run
- Visit the new beach
- Sell spare items at the new pawn shop
- Talk with the people you meet
- Venues open and close through the day
- Travel anywhere on the city map
- Earn an invite to the Grand Casino
- Your saved runs are protected now
- Lots of bug fixes and smoother play

Play free in the browser on itch.io.

Beat the House is simulated gambling only: no real-money wagering, no
cash prizes, no gambling monetization.
